Shipping Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Shipping Coverage

We currently ship to all locations within India. International shipping is not available at this time.

2. Delivery Timeline

  • Metro Cities: 3-5 business days
  • Other Cities: 5-7 business days
  • Remote Areas: 7-10 business days

Note: Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to unforeseen circumstances.

3. Shipping Charges

  • Free shipping on orders above ₹500
  • ₹50 shipping charge for orders below ₹500
  • Additional charges may apply for remote locations

4. Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days (Monday-Saturday, excluding public holidays). You'll receive a tracking number once your order is shipped.

5. Order Tracking

Track your order using:

  • Tracking link sent via email/SMS
  • Your account dashboard on our website
  • Contact our customer support

6. Delivery Attempts

Our courier partners will make 2-3 delivery attempts. If delivery fails, the package will be returned to us. You may need to place a new order or contact us for re-shipment (additional charges may apply).

7. Address Accuracy

Please ensure your shipping address is complete and accurate. We are not responsible for delays or non-delivery due to incorrect addresses.

8. Packaging

All products are carefully packaged to ensure safe delivery. If you receive a damaged package, please contact us immediately with photos.
