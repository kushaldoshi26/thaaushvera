Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Return Eligibility

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for:

  • Damaged or defective products
  • Wrong items delivered
  • Products not matching description

Products must be unused, in original packaging with all tags attached.

2. Non-Returnable Items

  • Opened or used products (for hygiene reasons)
  • Products without original packaging
  • Sale or clearance items

3. Return Process

  1. Contact our customer support within 7 days of delivery
  2. Provide order number and reason for return
  3. Ship the product back to our address (shipping costs may apply)
  4. Once received and inspected, we'll process your refund

4. Refund Timeline

Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after we receive and inspect the returned product. The amount will be credited to:

  • Original payment method (for online payments)
  • Bank account (for COD orders, upon providing bank details)

5. Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. Please return the product for a refund and place a new order for the desired item.

6. Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a damaged or defective product:

  • Contact us immediately with photos of the damage
  • We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund
  • Return shipping will be covered by us

7. Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled before shipping. Once shipped, you'll need to follow the return process. Refunds for cancelled orders are processed within 3-5 business days.
